Ingredients:
1 cup fine dessicated coconut
1 cup milk powder
1/2 cup milk
1/4th cup sugar
1 teaspoon cardamom powder
3-4 drops natural rose flavour
1/4th teaspoon natural pink food colour
Edible silver leaf and chopped pistachios for garnish
